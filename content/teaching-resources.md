## Building Your Own Resource Packs

Throughout the site you will find an **Add** button on individual resources such as nature examples, activities, handouts, and reflections. Clicking it adds that resource to your current resource pack. You can collect resources from across the site and then use the **Resource Pack** panel on the right-hand side to review, organise, edit, and save your collection, or generate a shareable link to send to colleagues or parents.

Resource packs are a flexible way to build your own tailored teaching materials — picking exactly the examples, activities, and handouts that are right for your class.

You can read more about resource packs here: [Building Your Own Resource Packs](/wiki/building-your-own-resource-packs).
